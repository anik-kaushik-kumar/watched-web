A psychological horror game that learns you

Game Vision

Not what you choose.
What you do.

WATCHED is built on a radical premise: the game is watching how you play — not what buttons you press, but how you move, hesitate, explore, and break rules.

Every mouse twitch, every idle second, every repeated corridor — it all feeds an invisible system that reshapes the world. The horror adapts to you specifically. No two players face the same game.

Design Pillars

The Foundation

01

Behavior-Driven

Player habits — not menu selections — reshape the world. Mouse speed, hesitation, route repetition, and rule-breaking are all tracked silently.

02

Adaptive Horror

The threat doesn't just get harder — it gets stranger. Chases change presentation. The world reacts to patterns. Difficulty is never a number.

03

NPC Psychology

NPCs don't help — they observe. Their dialogue escalates from casual remarks to deeply unsettling accusations, all reflecting real player behavior.

04

Indie-Realistic

Built for a solo developer or micro-team. No expensive AI. Psychological dread through systemic design, state machines, and intelligent reuse.

The Threat

The
Entity

It has no name. No fixed form. What it has is patience — and an appetite for understanding.

It doesn't just chase. It studies. Each encounter is an experiment. Each escape teaches it something new. By the final act, it knows you better than you know yourself.

Core Systems

How the game
sees you

Movement Profiling

Cursor speed, acceleration, and idle duration build a behavioral fingerprint.

Route Memory

Repeated paths trigger environmental shifts — subtle at first, then deeply wrong.

Obedience Index

Does the player follow safe options? Rule-breakers are treated as fundamentally different subjects.

Hesitation Detection

Pausing at doorways, hovering over objects, backing away — every micro-hesitation is logged.

Exploration vs. Objective

Lingering and searching vs. sprinting toward goals. The entity exploits whichever mode you operate in.

Endings

How It Ends

No ending choices. No dialogue trees. The game watches how you've played across the entire experience — and delivers the conclusion your behavior earned.

I

The Compliant

High obedience · Low exploration

You followed every instruction. Took every safe path. The Entity doesn't need to chase you at the end. It simply opens the door — and you walk through.

II

The Unraveled

High hesitation · Repeated routes

You circled. You returned. You couldn't commit. The world tightened around you — room by room — until the corridors you kept walking simply stopped leading anywhere new.

III

The Seen

Rule-breaking · Fast movement · High exploration

You ran. You broke rules. You pushed boundaries never meant to be found. The Entity stopped hiding. The final scene: a face-to-face confrontation. No escape.
